Research Interests

I am an interdisciplinary scientist studying the dynamic interactions between nanomaterials and biomolecules with the goal of leveraging nano-bio interfaces to propel the advancement of nanotechnologies that improve human health and enhance agricultural sustainability.


  • Nano-Omics

    Adsorption of biomolecules on nanomaterial surfaces, or nanomaterial biomolecular corona formation, can be leveraged as a nano-scale deep-profiling to profile proteomes and enrich biomarkers.

  • Corona-Mediated Nano-Sensors

    The biomolecular corona can be leveraged to develop efficient nano-sensors to detect early signatures, or biomarkers, in complex biological fluids and tissues.

  • Nanomaterial-Mediated Gene Delivery

    Nanotechnology, which adapts nanomaterials as nano-carriers, offers a sustainable solution to deliver biomolecules into cells and tissues. Developing these nanotechnologies based on nano-bio interfacial chemistry can improve their efficacy.
